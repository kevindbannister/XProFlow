# Professional Context taxonomy research (UK finance-first)

## Sources reviewed
- ICAEW and ACCA career pathways for practice progression (trainee → senior → manager → partner).
- CIMA/CGMA role guidance for in-house finance (management accountant, finance business partner, FP&A).
- CIPFA public/finance leadership role mapping (FD/CFO/Head of Finance) used broadly across UK organisations.
- GOV.UK / HMRC communications style expectations for tax-facing correspondence.
- UK job boards (Reed, Hays, Robert Half, Michael Page) for high-frequency title naming.

## Resulting taxonomy for pilot
1. **Primary role categories (single-select)**
   - Accountant (Practice)
   - Finance Director / CFO
   - Financial Controller
   - Finance Manager
   - Management Accountant / FP&A
   - Bookkeeper
   - Tax Advisor
   - Payroll Specialist
   - Practice Manager / Operations
   - Other

2. **Common job titles (searchable + custom)**
   - Practice examples: Partner, Managing Partner, Accounts Senior, Audit Manager, Tax Director, Practice Manager.
   - In-house examples: CFO, Finance Director, Head of Finance, Financial Controller, Finance Manager, Management Accountant, FP&A Manager.
   - Specialist examples: VAT Manager, Payroll Officer, Credit Controller, Treasury Manager, Insolvency Practitioner, Forensic Accountant.

3. **Seniority / authority (single-select)**
   - Owner/Partner
   - Director/Head of Finance
   - Manager
   - Senior/Qualified
   - Junior/Trainee/Assistant

4. **Work setting (single-select)**
   - Accounting Practice (public practice)
   - In-house / Industry
   - Fractional / Interim / Consultant
   - Outsourced finance provider / bureau
   - Other

5. **Specialisms (multi-select)**
   - Accounts prep / statutory accounts; Bookkeeping; Management accounts; FP&A / budgeting / forecasting; VAT; Payroll; Corporation tax; Personal tax; Audit; Advisory / CFO services; Credit control; Treasury / cashflow; Finance business partnering; Insolvency / restructuring; Forensic / investigations; Systems / process improvement.

6. **Typical audiences (multi-select)**
   - Business owners / directors; Non-finance client contacts; Internal finance team; Wider internal stakeholders; HMRC / regulators; Banks / lenders; Suppliers; Other advisors.

7. **Tone + risk posture (single-select each)**
   - Writing style: Very formal, Professional (default), Plain English.
   - Risk posture: Compliance-first, Balanced (default), Commercial.

8. **Jurisdiction defaults**
   - Locale default set to `en-GB`.
   - Prompt guidance includes UK spelling and HMRC-aligned phrasing.

## Pilot notes
- Taxonomy is intentionally compact for sub-3-minute completion.
- US-only title variants excluded unless common in UK postings.
